BRISTOL BEER FACTORY

BBF (Bristol Beer Factory) started life in 2004, brewing just a few standard cask conditioned ales in a vibrant part of south Bristol.

Since then production has developed to offer a range of different regular bottled ales, cask ales and a variety of interesting specials throughout the year.

BBF are proud to be part of the current ‘craft beer revolution’ and do their utmost to persuade, convince and tell everyone they meet what they should be drinking.
