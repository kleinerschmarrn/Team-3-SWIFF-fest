DANNY'S BURGERS


Danny's Burgers started as a passion project off the back of winning Grillstock 2017. Danny started making his signature cali style high grade double smash burgers during the first lock down. Running out of a fierce and noble brewery in St Werburghs Bristol, he started to grow a loyal customer base, which led to his winning Burger Chef of the Year 2021 and UK's Best Burger 2022 at the National Burger Awards.
