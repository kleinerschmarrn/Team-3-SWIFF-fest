DHAMAKA

Dhamaka showcases the authentic street food of India - the food that Indians have savoured for all these years and has never become old fashioned.

The curries, some not so commonly heard of, will tickle your taste buds and take you down the streets of India – the kebabs of Lucknow, chat food from Bombay, the Hyderabadi biryanis, Delhi’s butter chicken, Keralan cheman mappas, and infinitely many more.
