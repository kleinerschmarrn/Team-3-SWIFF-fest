SWOON GELATO

Swoon Gelato was founded by the Forte family: Bruno, his wife Ana Maria, mother Louise, and sister Patricia. Together, they have brought premium gelato to the UK, which won an unprecedented 22 stars at the 2019 Great Taste Awards (often described as “the food and drink Oscars”).

They still use traditional methods to slowly churn the gelato, as well as high quality, natural ingredients, to create the finest tasting gelato in a range of different flavours.
